T.R.A.C.K. About Page

Hello fellow students! My name is DJ. I am a college and high school tutor, personal trainer, IT worker, and lifelong college student. I have nearly six associates degrees in majors such as liberal arts, kinesiology, business and computer information systems from Cabrillo College. I also transferred to San Jose State University to complete my upper-level general education. Now I hope to finish my BBA in Information Systems Management from Brandman Univeristy by the Summer of 2022. I realized that the medical and technology fields are growing and in constant need of tutoring and online reference. I created this website to assist my tutees and other students with similar scholastic pursuits.

*Remember that this website is intended for educational purposes only. The site was created by a college student who wishes to share their research, notes, hints, examples, and findings with others to help other students learn material faster and remember the material longer. Please request information from a professional, academic, or government source before making any important health, financial, legal, or technical decisions.
